International Forum


The International Forum(FI) is the second event of the year.



Speakers of the foreign schools come to describe their school. Students of 1A,2A and 3A can also find a lot of

information needed for their departure abroad.



Thus, students organize themselves to do the 12 weeks abroad their studies within the school that is in the

form of internship, double degree, a semester abroad or a summer job.



Thanks to our team and our guide, no need to search on internet: students have all the necessary information



If you are interested, join us!

Business Forum


The Business Forum(BF) is one of the first events of the year.



It allows students to:

- 4A: find their 4-month internship at the end of the year (from April to July inclusive)

- 5A: find their final year internship of 6 months(from January to June) and get their degree and a future job



Many companies (about 70 this year) send their representatives to contact the students (about 300) through

various workshops:

- Conferences

- The job dating

- The exibition



Thanks to our team and our guide, no need to search on internet: students have all the necessary information

about the organization of the day and the companies



So join us and enjoy this day !
